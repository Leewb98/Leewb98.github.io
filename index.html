About me

I am a PhD student at the Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology (SIAT), part of the Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), where I am dedicated to the advancement of PET (Positron Emission Tomography) imaging and analysis. My research endeavors to integrate sophisticated deep learning methodologies to refine PET imaging resolution and concurrently mitigate the risks associated with radiation exposure. This cutting-edge work is poised to revolutionize medical diagnostics by delivering superior image quality and safer patient experiences.
